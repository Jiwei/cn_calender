XIALI Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

Overview

XIALI - 中国农历 is a Chinese Lunisolar Calendar browser extension. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the extension handles.

Data Collection

XIALI collects no personal data. The extension:

  • Does not collect, store, or transmit any user data
  • Does not make any network requests
  • Does not use cookies or local storage
  • Does not track usage or analytics

Permissions

XIALI requires no browser permissions. All calendar calculations are performed locally within the extension popup.

Third Parties

XIALI does not share any data with third parties because no data is collected.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please open an issue at github.com/jiwei/cn_calender.
